HTML links are hyperlinks. You can click on a link and jump to another document . When you move on the mouse over a link the mouse arrow will turn into a little hand symbol.

By default, links will appear as follows in all browsers:

1. an univisited link is underlined and blue
2. a visited link is underlined and purple
3. an active link is underlined and red.

HTML links - the target attribut.

By default the linked page will be displed in the current browser window. to change the this problem you nmust specify antother target for the link.


THE target atteibute can have one of the values:
_self
_blank
_parent
_top
